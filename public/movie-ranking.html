Cole's Top Movies

My Top 3 All-Time Movies

#1. Lone Survivor

My favorite movie based on the true story of Navy Seals Mike Murphy and Marcus Lattrell. Peter Berg directs the movie that stars Mark Wahlberg and Taylor Klitsch.

#2. John Wick

Action, action, and more action! This movie features Keanu Reeves as John Wick on a revenge tour for Russian mobsters killing John's dog. John's dog was a gift from his late wife, the last gift she left for him. John doesn't stop until every last person associated with his dog's killing, is gone.

#3. Remember the Titans

Denzel Washington stars in this movie that takes place during the Civil Rights Era of the United States. His football team is the first to assemble players of Black and White races. He's tasked with getting these players to trust and believe in each other when the rest of the town does not agree with the assimilation in the local high school.
